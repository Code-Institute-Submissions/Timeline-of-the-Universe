Blessed Tidings To You Weary Traveller!

Here is my mortal attempt at capturing a mere figment of the wonderous dimensional plane we like to call:

THE UNIVERSE


Navigate the interactive timeline by clicking the Left and Right arrows at either side of the main image, or click and drag the Timeline itself to find timepoints you would like to click and view!

The Timeline displays time as BYA (Billion Years Ago) and MYA (Million Years Ago).

Each era in the Universe's existance has its own timeline page, which can be found on the top navigation bar.

Enjoy exploring!
