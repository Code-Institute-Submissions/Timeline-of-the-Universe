Welcome to Universalis Extraordinaire!

Here is my mortal attempt at capturing a mere glimpse of the wonderous void we like to call:

THE UNIVERSE

Navigate the interactive timeline by clicking the Left and Right arrows at either side of the main image, or click and drag the Timeline itself to find timepoints you would like to click and view!

The Timeline displays time as BYA (Billion Years Ago) and MYA (Million Years Ago).

Each era of the Universe's existance has its own timeline page, which can be found on the top navigation bar. Once you've reached the last slide of a Timeline, use this navigation bar to move to the next era.

The Age of Man section is still under construction so is currently inaccessible

Enjoy exploring!
